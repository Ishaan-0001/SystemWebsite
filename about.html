About SY-STEM

Who we are

A student-led non-profit on a mission to make STEM accessible, exciting, and meaningful for every young learner.

Our Story

Born from a simple belief

SY-STEM was founded by a group of high school students who noticed something troubling: the way science and math were being taught wasn't working. Students were disengaged, curious minds were stifled, and learning felt like punishment.

So we did something about it. We started hosting small labs and tutoring groups — and the response was overwhelming. Kids who had given up on science were suddenly excited again.

That's when we knew SY-STEM needed to grow into something bigger.

What We Do

Learning by doing

Every SY-STEM program is built around hands-on experience. Our sessions cover chemistry experiments, physics demos, coding workshops, and math puzzles.

We work with students from kindergarten through 6th grade, meeting them at their level. All instructors are passionate high school and college students who remember what it felt like to be inspired by science for the first time.
